summary: |
  Strong Cr–N covalency stabilises low-spin S = ½ Cr³⁺ in Ca₃CrN₃,
  producing quasi-1D spin chains with a very large nearest-neighbour
  antiferromagnetic exchange J = 340 K and no magnetic order down to
  0.1 K — a rare experimental realisation of a strongly quantum spin
  chain in an oxide-free environment.
