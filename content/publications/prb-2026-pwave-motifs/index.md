summary: |
  Identifies a class of compensated antiferromagnets in which antiparallel
  spins occupy symmetry-inequivalent sites related by a mirror operation —
  enabling a magnetoelectric coupling that produces p-wave off-centered
  Fermi surfaces and intrinsic spin / crystal / anomalous Hall effects
  locked to the mirror plane. Five prototypical candidates identified,
  including the high-pressure triple perovskite Mn₄Nb₂O.
