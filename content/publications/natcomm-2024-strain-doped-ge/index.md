abstract: |
  Bulk Ge is an indirect-bandgap semiconductor, blocking efficient on-chip
  light emission on silicon. We propose and demonstrate a *strain-doping* route
  that drives Ge into a true direct-bandgap regime by exploiting the unified
  semiconductor band-gap theory developed in our earlier work. The result is a
  CMOS-compatible photonic emitter without epitaxial integration of III–Vs.
summary: |

  strain-doping — an industry-relevant route to silicon-compatible on-chip
  light sources. Patent filed (US Patent Application No. 16/760,863).
